Alexa Boise Tour

Description:


  • Embark on a captivating journey through downtown Boise with our cutting-edge Alexa application, "Alexa Boise Tour."
  • Immerse yourself in the vibrant pulse of the city as you explore iconic landmarks such as JUMP, where creativity knows no bounds, and the lively Basque Block, a cultural hub brimming with history and flavor.
  • Brace yourself for an adrenaline rush at Freak Outly, an exhilarating stop that promises thrills and excitement.
  • Lastly, lose yourself in the eclectic atmosphere of Treefort, where music, arts, and community converge in an unforgettable celebration.
  • Let Alexa Boise Tour be your guide, unlocking hidden gems and local treasures at every turn.
  • Prepare for an immersive experience that transcends the ordinary, as we unveil the heart and soul of downtown Boise like never before.
