Project T.A.i.R. (Tetris Attack in Roblox)

Welcome to the development log for Project T.A.i.R., my complete recreation of the classic puzzle game Tetris Attack, built entirely within the Roblox engine.

Gameplay Showcase (Video to be filled in)

Development Journey

Building Project T.A.i.R. has been an incredible technical and creative journey. I have successfully been able to complete the core gameplay loop, ensuring that the fast-paced puzzle mechanics, combo systems, and chains feel just as snappy and satisfying as the original game. Bringing a nostalgic, competitive experience to a platform like Roblox required a lot of precise fine-tuning to perfectly match the original game's physics and state management.

Monetization and Game Philosophy

However, one of the biggest struggles I've faced during this process was trying to turn the game into a source of income. It's been extremely difficult trying to adapt my creation into a "money-grabbing" Roblox game, as the platform's ecosystem often encourages and heavily rewards aggressive monetization mechanics like loot boxes or pay-to-win advantages.

Instead of compromising the integrity of the game with a pay-to-win motive and watching players inevitably leave, I've turned my attention the other way. My primary focus is now on adding as much value, polish, and fun as I possibly can. I want to build a game that makes people genuinely want to come back because they love the gameplay experience. Quality and long-term player retention are my highest priorities—because a great, fair game speaks for itself!
